What is Our Solution?

We believe Africa needs a standardized health data infrastructure that will allow seamless integration of different information such as genomic data, clinical data, pharmacy data, and diagnostic images in order to give clinicians a longitudinal, full picture of their patient’s health in order to make the best possible diagnosis and treatment decisions. That’s why we are building BiodataHero – Africa’s first blockchain based healthcare data management and exchange platform for secure storage, collaboration and sharing of patients’ records so that clinicians and researchers who need this data most can access it all at the point of care. This will profoundly improve patient care coordination, quality and efficiency. Starting with Cancer, we will build our platform to help oncologists integrate, structure and analyse health data from disparate sources (clinical, imaging and genomic data) and apply machine leaning algorithms to identify patterns and create a precise profile of the cancer to enable the right treatment decision for each patient, thereby driving better patient outcomes.
